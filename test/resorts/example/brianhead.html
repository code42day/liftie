Road Conditions

Brian Head Resort has the highest base elevation in Utah. During storms the highways can become dangerous. Click here to visit Utah Department of Transportation’s current road conditions. You can also reach their 24 hour phone line at 866-511-8824 or just 511 if calling from a phone with a Utah area code.
