Snow Report

Status update for Thu, Nov 26

1:25 PM

Happy Thanksgiving and welcome to Winter 2015-2016 at Sugarbush. We've had a great day of soft fun turns, thanks to our awesome snowmakers and groomers. Friday's forecast calls for cloudy skies and temps in the mid 40's to low 50's top to bottom. 

We have intermediate and expert terrain open currently, with tasty groomer turns on Upper Jester, Downspout and Allyn's Traverse. Organgrinder will remain ungroomed, except for the bottom section which will be rolled tonight. Sugarbush Parks has a sweet feature garden on Allyn's Traverse. 

Super Bravo and Heaven's Gate lifts are scheduled to spin 9AM-3PM through the weekend, with downloading on Super Bravo.

There is still time to enjoy the Timbers' Thanksgiving Buffet. Play all day, while our chefs prepare a tasty turkey feast for you. We'll handle clean up too, you just put your feet up and smile.

We're excited to share a new Valley House Quad this season. Also new for 2015-16, Mt. Ellen's Green Mountain Express Quad will open at 8 AM daily, making before-work turns more accessible.

The Season Pass and Ticket Office is open from 9AM - 4PM, for season pass pickup and sales. 

Our famous Quad Pack deal is on sale until the end of November. Get 4 unrestricted adult all mountain lifts tickets for just $229, good any time throughout the season. SugarDirect and Advance Purchase Tickets are also available. Our mid-week Boomer Pass (65+) is only $199. If you’re lucky enough to be in your 20’s, you can get unlimited skiing and riding for only $459. 

Please remember the mountain is closed while we prepare for winter. Check our Winter Trail Use Policy for more information.

Get all the details at www.sugarbush.com and keep in touch on our Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and Google+ pages. Be better here.
