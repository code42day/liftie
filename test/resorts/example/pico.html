Welcome to the Snow Report

Current Conditions

(as of Wednesday, November 25, 2015)

Pico Mountain is now closed for lift-served skiing and riding. We would like to thank everyone who came out to ski and snowboard with us this season, it was a great one and we couldn't have done it without you. Thanks also to everyone who participated in our opening and closing weekend food drives and to BROC-Community Action in Southwest Vermont for the important work they do. See you all next season.

Don't forget that 2015-16 Pico Season Passes are on sale now. Current 2014-15 and activated 2015-16 Season Pass holders are eligible for a $29 all ages lift ticket at Killington Resort from April 6 through April 17, just by presenting their pass at the ticket window. Pico Pass holders ar also eligible to save $20 on a Killington Spring Pass. Please note the discounted ticket is for the pass holder only, not friends or family.

Beginning on Saturday, May 2, 2015, the Pico Sports Center shifts to summer hours. They are: Monday-Friday 5:30 a.m. - 8:00 p.m., Saturday 8:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m., and Sunday 8:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.
