Today: Wednesday November 25, 2015

Updated Wednesday November 25, 2015 7:12 AM

Todays Hours

Comments

Snowmaking has continued since Sunday evening with favorable temperatures that have allowed us to keep piling up the snow. We'll keep working at it every chance possible for our projected opening of Saturday, December 5. Check out our trail list to see where the guns are going. It's our usual early-season trails and once those are good to go we'll continue moving to make the connection between the two mountains as soon as we can. Stay tuned, we'll keep you updated on our progress as we turn this mountain from green to white.

If you want to be skiing and riding today, tomorrow Thanksgiving Day or over the holiday weekend and in to next week, be sure to head on up to our nearby sister ski area, Wildcat Mountain. They are open for skiing and riding today, Wednesday, November 25 with top-to-bottom terrain and will be operating daily, so be sure to check out the Wildcat Snow Report for the latest.

Make sure to check out the Vertical Value Card (on sale now!) for savings on lift tickets all season long as well as additional perks. We've got Gift Cards for sale which make great stocking stuffers and can be used at both Attitash and Wildcat. There's also some great seasonal programs lined up for this season so be sure to explore our new website to see what our ski school can offer to you this season.

If you are looking to pick up your season pass, our guest service/season pass office is currently open Monday - Saturday 9 a.m. - 3 p.m., but will be closed on Thanksgiving Day.
